Toronto is home to Canada's major employers and the epicenter of most of the country's key industries. 
The provincial capital of Ontario is one of the most diverse cities that provides students with access to a rich social and cultural heritage. 
In the center of the metropolis, George Brown College is ideally located - one of the largest public colleges in Canada. GBC's state-of-the-art classrooms and laboratories simulate a real work environment. Many faculty members have industry experience and share their practical knowledge with students. 


Most programs offer students an applied education with the opportunity to gain Canadian work experience, establish work relationships and learn from professionals.
